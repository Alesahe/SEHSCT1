Astral Projections

~ an astronomical calendar of sorts ~

Welcome!

Astral Projections is a free, open-source, all-in-one calendar for your astronomical needs!



Features and Capabilities

  • displays relevant information on upcoming astronomical events!
  • provides photos of recent events to share with the community!
  • with permission, notifies you on the day of the event!
  • offers an easy-to-use search bar!


Interface, Inclusivity and Accessibility Considerations

  • text is able to be read by screenreaders
  • reading mode / reading view is supported
  • frequent use of headings to structure content
  • alternative text for images
  • high-contrast colours
  • clear, easily identifiable error messages
  • keyboard accessibilty (using Tab)
  • readable fonts (serifs)
  • text and website resizability (Ctrl + +)
  • visible indication of focus
